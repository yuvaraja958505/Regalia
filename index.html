Redefining Luxury Living

Regalia presents an exclusive collection of ultra-luxury residences in the heart of Indiranagar, Bengaluru. Meticulously designed to offer an unparalleled living experience, Regalia combines sophisticated architecture with world-class amenities.


Spread across 4.5 acres of prime real estate, the project features only 120 exclusive residences, ensuring privacy and exclusivity. Designed by award-winning architects and built by RL Groups, one of Bengaluru's most trusted developers.


Every detail at Regalia has been carefully curated to offer residents a lifestyle of comfort, convenience, and luxury. From the grand entrance lobby to the meticulously landscaped gardens, no expense has been spared in creating a truly exceptional living environment.
